Privacy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

We place great importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how the Noctua application collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our application. It applies to all Noctua users, whether you reside in France, the European Union, the United States, or any other country.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Noctua, based in Paris, France.

Email: contact@noctua-app.com

2. Data Collected

2.1 Profile Data

Data Required Purpose
First name No Experience personalization
Last name No Experience personalization
Email address Email OR phone required Account management
Phone number Email OR phone required Account management
Gender No Content personalization
Discovery source No Service improvement

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Anonymous user identifier (UUID)
  • Timezone and language
  • Device type and OS
  • Application version

2.3 Usage and Analytics Data

  • App opens and usage frequency
  • Onboarding progression
  • Quote interactions
  • Search queries
  • Daily journeys completed
  • Ad impressions

2.4 Diagnostic Data

Via Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob:

  • Crash data
  • Performance data
  • Other diagnostic data

Collected anonymized.

2.5 Advertising Identifiers

  • iOS (IDFA): Only with consent via ATT.
  • Android: Managed by Google Play Services.

5. Consent and Advertising

5.1 GDPR (EU)

Consent is managed via the Google UMP (User Messaging Platform). You can change your preferences at any time in the app settings.

5.2 ATT (iOS)

A system prompt requests access to the IDFA. You can decline.

5.3 CCPA (California)

You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal data.

7. International Data Transfers

Data transfers outside of the EU are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

8. Data Security

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • JWT authentication
  • Least privilege access
  • Access monitoring
  • SOC 2 certified hosting

10. Push Notifications

A daily notification is sent at the time you choose. Your last activity is recorded. You can disable notifications at any time.

11. Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • User preferences
  • Profile (offline access)
  • Daily journey status
  • Notification settings

This data is not transmitted except during synchronization.

12. Children's Privacy

Noctua is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not intentionally collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us at contact@noctua-app.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

When this policy is updated, the date at the top of this page will be modified. You will be notified via the app. Re-consent will be requested if necessary.

14. Complaints to a Supervisory Authority

  • France: CNIL (www.cnil.fr)
  • Other EU: Your local Data Protection Authority
  • UK: ICO (ico.org.uk)
  • US: FTC (ftc.gov)
